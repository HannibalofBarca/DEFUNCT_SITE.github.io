 Aditya Birla Insulators Internship

 Designs for three railways metal parts were created for FEM analysis and weight optimization.
 Approximately, the weight of all parts was reduced by 20% without any significant reduction in structural integrity.
 All three parts are now in production.

 Atomic Force Microscope Cantilever

 An AFM cantilever based on "Simulation of vibrational resonances of stiff AFM cantilevers by finite element methods" was created and parametrically defined in SolidWorks.
 Further FEM and modal simulations were carried out in Ansys.
